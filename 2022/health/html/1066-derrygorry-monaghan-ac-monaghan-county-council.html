1 Key Points

  • Derrygorry, Monaghan covers a total area of 14.6 square kilometers and has a population density of 20 persons per square kilometer. The population density of Ireland as a whole is 73.3.

  • The Age Dependency Ratio of this Electoral Division (ED) is 56.1. This compares to 53.2 nationally. The Age Dependency Ratio is the amount of people outside of working age (0-14 and 65+) per 100 people of working age (15-64).

  • Derrygorry, Monaghan has a total of 292 persons and 78 families living in private households.

  • There are a total of 62 persons aged 0-14 in this ED and 43 persons aged over 65.

  • There are a total of 50 persons living with a disability in this ED, representing 17.1 percent of the population. This compares with , 21.5, percent nationally

  • 6 people in this ED have bad or very bad general health. This represents 2 percent of the population. 1.7 percent of the population have bad or very bad general health nationally.

  • There are a total of 11 carers in this ED, representing 3.8 percent of the population. This compares with 5.8 percent of the population that are carers nationally.

  • 7.5 percent of this ED smoke tobacco products. 13.1 percent of people nationally smoke tobacco products

  • There are 4 short term unemployed and 7 long term unemployed people in this ED. These represent 1.7 and 3.0 percent of the population respectively.

  • 2.4 percent of the population of Derrygorry, Monaghan are unskilled. 3.1 percent of people in the state are unskilled.

  • 33.2 percent of those aged 15+ in this ED have a highest level of education of lower secondary or lower. This compares with 23 percent nationally.

  • 9.6 percent of families with children in this ED have lone parents. This stands at 24.8 nationally.

  • 27.5 percent of this ED were born outside Ireland (20 percent nationally).

  • Households rented from a Local Authority comprise 3.0 percent of households in this ED.This stands at 8.3 percent nationally
