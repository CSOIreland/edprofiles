1 Key Points

  • Killeagh, Cork County, 047202 covers a total area of 23.6 square kilometers and has a population density of 67.3 persons per square kilometer. The population density of Ireland as a whole is 73.3.

  • The Age Dependency Ratio of this Electoral Division (ED) is 57.5. This compares to 53.2 nationally. The Age Dependency Ratio is the amount of people outside of working age (0-14 and 65+) per 100 people of working age (15-64).

  • Killeagh, Cork County, 047202 has a total of 1588 persons and 381 families living in private households.

  • There are a total of 386 persons aged 0-14 in this ED and 194 persons aged over 65.

  • There are a total of 320 persons living with a disability in this ED, representing 20.2 percent of the population. This compares with , 21.5, percent nationally

  • 22 people in this ED have bad or very bad general health. This represents 1.4 percent of the population. 1.7 percent of the population have bad or very bad general health nationally.

  • There are a total of 105 carers in this ED, representing 6.6 percent of the population. This compares with 5.8 percent of the population that are carers nationally.

  • 11.5 percent of this ED smoke tobacco products. 13.1 percent of people nationally smoke tobacco products

  • There are 12 short term unemployed and 20 long term unemployed people in this ED. These represent 1.0 and 1.7 percent of the population respectively.

  • 2.1 percent of the population of Killeagh, Cork County, 047202 are unskilled. 3.1 percent of people in the state are unskilled.

  • 20.8 percent of those aged 15+ in this ED have a highest level of education of lower secondary or lower. This compares with 23 percent nationally.

  • 15.3 percent of families with children in this ED have lone parents. This stands at 24.8 nationally.

  • 11 percent of this ED were born outside Ireland (20 percent nationally).

  • Households rented from a Local Authority comprise 6.7 percent of households in this ED.This stands at 8.3 percent nationally
